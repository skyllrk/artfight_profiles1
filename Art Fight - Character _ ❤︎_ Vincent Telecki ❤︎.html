Personality

Vincent is very confident and dominant, unlikely to compromise unless he's forced to. He has natural charm which he uses to his advantage. His "media" persona is carefully curated, leaving no space for showing any weakness. Due to his status as both CEO and a face of the Network he's under a lot of pressure to maintain the perfect facade. However at home he's a completely different person.


At home, Vincent finally gets to unmask and be himself. He often rants about his work or generally anything that bothers him to his husband, who is a good listener. He is very temperamental and prone to outbursts but he tries his best to work on it.
As a partner Vincent is very caring. Due to being more experienced one in their relationship, he feels responsible for Altie and his happiness. Vincent spares no expense on his husband. He e is prone to feeling a bit insecure and worrying too much about if this love will last (after all he is a very flawed man and what if Altie one day walks out?). However everytime Altie notices that, he quickly reasures him that no matter what they will stay together forever and that he isn't with him due to his fame, good looks, money; he loves Vincent for who he is and he's dedicated to keep reminding him that for the rest of eternity.

Backstory

Vincent knew that he was trans since a very young age. He didn't have a happy family life, often being forced to perform, being treated more like a trophy than a kid. That's all he is willing to share,talking about his past makes him visibily distressed and he will do anything to avoid the subject.
Vincent started as a weather presenter at the TV station, continuosly climbing his way through the corporate ladder until one day he became the owner of the network and renaming it to "VoxTV" which continued growing to become the #1 watched channel in Poland. Currently the channel is a part of "VoxMedia" newtork along multiple thematic channels such as VoxDiscovery; VoxHistory; VoxKit; VoxTV24 and many more.
One day he met a special someone...


That fateful day, Vincent was on the way to his office getting on the elavator. The very last minute as the doors were about to close,an unfamiliar younger man made it inside bumping into Vincent. Instead of immediately feeling intimated meeting the CEO (especially in this circumstance), the guy introoduced himself enthusiasthically and casually struck a conversation with Vincent as he noticed the shark pin on his tuxedo. Apparently Altie was also a big fan of shark. At first Vincent was a bit taken aback by the audacity of Altie, but that was exactly the thing that really caught his attention - a newbie who didn't care about the status or power, simply being friendly for the sake of being friendly. Later that day Altie's manager hearing about all this came to Vincent's office to apologize or his new hire and insisting that he will fire for this disrespect. However much to his surprise Vincent promoted the New Hire to his personal assistant (after all it was the easisest way to get to know Altie). Gradually by working together they realised they were meant to be and a few months later Vincent proposed to Altie and they got married. Since then both of their lives changed for the better.

Relationships
Altie - Husband

There's quite a few things Vincent absolutely adores in Altie. First and foremost it's definitely how he doesn't care about others opinions and is being true to himself.Altie is also a great listener and amazing at cheering Vincent up, he's also extremely patient when it comes to reassuring Vincent and only when it comes to Vincent, otherwise Altie is incredibly impatient. His recklessness is what frustrates Vincent the most. Altie tends to do something without a thought.


Vincent loves taking Altie out for fun dates, whether it's the museum, aquarium with sharks, restaurant, beach or simply movie night in a pillow fort they both would build, Vincent is always in awe seeing how excited and happy Altie is for spending time together. Cuddling with his husband or simply holding him in his arms is the most precious thing for Vincent and he wouldn't trade it for anything.
